My University


I am a student at Royal University of Phnom Penh. My university is located in Toul Kork District, Phnom Penh City.

There are many departments of major skills in my university's area. Its area is absolutely huge. Its has 3 lakes and more than 5 bike parking-lots. There are many classrooms.

My major skill is Computer Science and Engineers. Now, I am a junior (Year 4).

My university was built for about 60 years ago. It is the oldest university in Cambodia. There is also history of this university. It was famous in 1960s. However, it is still well-known in these days.
